My Process

A simple process that protects the user and the business goal.

Recruiters and clients need to see more than pretty visuals. This section shows how you think before you design.

01

Diagnose

I audit the current experience, review user behavior, map friction points, and define the business objective.

02

Structure

I create flows, wireframes, content hierarchy, page logic, and decision paths before moving into polished UI.

03

Design

I design responsive interfaces with strong spacing, typography, trust signals, visual hierarchy, and clear CTAs.

04

Prototype

I connect screens into clickable prototypes so stakeholders can understand flow, interaction, and user intent.

05

Validate

I use feedback, analytics, heatmaps, recordings, and usability notes to reduce guesswork and improve decisions.

06

Handoff

I prepare clean files, components, annotations, responsive rules, and developer-ready specs for implementation.
